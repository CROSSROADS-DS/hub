<section class="page-section">
  <div class="container content">
    <h2>How modules are organized</h2>
    <p>Each public module is maintained as an independent repository. A module may include a notebook, data, images, a README, metadata, documentation, and licensing information.</p>

    <h2>Public and instructor materials</h2>
    <p>Student-facing and openly shareable materials are published in public repositories. Instructor resources—such as solutions, rubrics, assessments, lesson plans, and teaching notes—are maintained separately in private companion repositories.</p>

    <h2>Licensing and reuse</h2>
    <p>Consult the license in each module repository before adapting or redistributing its contents. Module-level licensing information takes precedence over general descriptions on this Hub.</p>

    <h2>Getting started</h2>
    <ol>
      <li>Browse the <a href="{{ '/modules/' | relative_url }}">module catalog</a>.</li>
      <li>Open a module repository and review its README and materials.</li>
      <li>Use the <a href="https://github.com/orgs/crossroads-ds/discussions">Forum</a> to ask questions, share experiences, or suggest ideas.</li>
    </ol>

    <div class="notice">
      <strong>Future guidance:</strong> A full Builder's Guide and Platform Starter Kit are planned for later stages. They will explain adaptation, contribution, instructor access, and platform setup in greater depth.
